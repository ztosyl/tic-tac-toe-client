Instructions

Welcome to tic tac toe! In order to win, you must get three of your letter (either X or O) in the same space. Click on a space to claim it for you letter. X goes first, and then O goes and does the same. Proceed like this until the game is over.

However! This version has MAGIC SPACES: the Exploding space and the Switch space. Place your letter on the Exploding space and you instantly lose! Place your letter on the Switch space and all Xs and Os on the board switch spaces!

There are no ties. If it looks like you are about to tie, both players must keep choosing until one chooses the Exploding space.

Good luck! Click on the board to make the first move and begin the game, or click on 'Restart Game' to start a new one.
